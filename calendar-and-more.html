Calendar and More

This server is available to the students in Music 47, for free.
It is intended to stay online during Winter quarter and Spring break, 2021.
There is no guarantee of up time, and no 24/7 support is provided.
This was built as a research project, and you should use it being mindful of that.

To avoid overloading the server I'd like to invite the interested users to use the calendar.
You can sign up for a time slot using your @uci.edu google account. But please, keep in mind that:
- your email address might become visible to other users who use this calendar.
